Walleco - Expense Tracker

Currently in closed testing (not publicly available on Google Play).

Take control of your finances with Walleco – an all-in-one tool for expense tracking and budget planning. Easily manage your income and spending, set saving goals, and analyze your financial habits with advanced statistics and clear visualizations.

Key Features:

  • Track expenses and income – record daily transactions with ease.
  • Budgets and goals – set personal limits and financial objectives.
  • Advanced statistics – get detailed insights and smart financial reports.
  • Charts and visualizations – understand money flow with intuitive graphs.
  • Daily streak – build healthy financial habits step by step.
  • Cloud sync – securely store your data and access it anytime, anywhere.
  • CSV support – import and export your data effortlessly.
  • Profiles and settings – personalize your experience with multiple profiles.

With Walleco, you can better manage your money, cut unnecessary spending, and achieve financial goals faster. Whether you need a simple expense tracker or a complete financial management tool – Walleco has everything you need in one app.
